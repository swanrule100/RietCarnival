Guidelines that keep the fun fair and the vibes great

General Rules

For the Registration in Sports events captain is allowed to Register with their Team.

Individual is allowed to play in a particular team in single sport.

Every Team captain should join their respective WhatsApp group by the link provided in Google form (Only captain can join).

It is Responsibility of team captain to call his team on ground 5min before their match timing.

If a team fails to show up on ground on the given time, then opponent team will be considered as the winner of that particular match.

Any type of indiscipline or misbehavior can lead to disqualification of team.

Any type of arguing, disrespectful behavior with the match referee or umpire can lead to disqualification.

Only the registered players can play the sport, any type of change is allowed in any situation during the Event.

If any team walk of ground on protest during the playing time the team will be disqualified from the tournament.
