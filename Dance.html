BASKETBALL

Game Rules

  • There should be 5 players in a team
  • A single match will last over 20 minutes, it carries four quarters of 5 minutes.
  • After every quarter there will be a timeout for 45 seconds.
  • There are some basic fouls which will be followed throughout the game like dribble foul, back court, carrying a ball, pivot foot foul, Back-Tap etc.
  • Any physical act while dribbling or snatching the ball from offensive team member if it is done to hurt opponent player and to get the ball intentionally. The referee will be in charge. of any action according to him.
  • A blocking foul is called when one player uses their body to prevent the movement of another player.
