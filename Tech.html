LOGO DESIGN

Game Rules

  • All team should have 2 members NO SOLO, or more than 2 will be allowed.
  • You'll will be given a problem statement each, which will consist of company name, its working sector and client specifics
  • Based on your problems a time of 2 Hrs will be given to create a Logo for the company.
  • You may use any software of your choice, but the final result must be submitted in SVC format.
  • Plagiarism will be checked by the coordinator's, so don't copy anything from the internet. However you may take reference from the net.
  • You may bring your own system, however systems and internet will be provided.

SHORT FILM MAKING

Game Rules

  • Showcase your acting, direction, shooting, editing talent in this event with a video of not more than 7 mins.
  • Each team will be given a time period of 3 Hours to submit their work,
  • You may use any device of your choice and compatibility(mobile phone, camera etc.).
  • The video must be shot inside of the College Campus and it must be appropriate according to the theme provided to a particular team/individual.
  • You can use an software to edit the Video.
  • Final result is to be submitted in Mp4 Format for evaluation.
  • Please take concern of whom so ever you may shoot, organizing committee will not hold any responsibility in case of a dispute.

BRIDGE MANIA

Game Rules

  • In this event you need to construct a bridge using Ice-Cream Sticks and Glue.
  • Your bridge may be a simulation of real world structures.
  • Your team may be asked about the idea and thought process about the construction method/way your team might be following.
  • Equipment's for event will be provided by the organizers.
  • Only solo and duo will be allowed.
  • Time will be announced on the moment.
  • Winners will be chosen on the basis of load bearing capacity by the Judges.
